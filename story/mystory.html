Welcome to my cruise trip to Mexico

Back in 2019, my family and I went on a 7 day cruise taking us through the gulf of mexico to one of the seven wonders of the world, Chichén Itzá. Our adventure start with a long road trip from Newmarket all the way to Detroit, USA. We flew from Detroit down to New Orleans where the cruise ship was docked. The ship then took us down the gulf of mexico and to Yucatán, Mexico.
